Common Questions

Clear answers to help you move forward.

What does Mashhor Hub do?

Mashhor Hub is a premium Kuwaiti marketing platform that integrates influencer campaigns, brand identity, digital advertising, bilingual web platforms, video & content production, and AI-powered operations to help brands grow across Kuwait, the GCC, and the Arab world.

Do you work in both Arabic and English?

Yes. Every service we offer is delivered natively in both Arabic and English — not just translated, but authentically crafted for each language with genuine cultural understanding and market-specific messaging.

Is Mashhor Hub only for influencer marketing?

No. Influencer and celebrity campaigns are one of our core services, but we also offer brand identity, graphic design, digital advertising, web development, video production, content creation, AI automation, and strategic consulting — all as an integrated system.

How do projects usually start?

Most projects begin with a free strategy call where we understand your business goals and challenges. Then we recommend the right service mix or package, scope the project, and begin execution — often within days of approval.

What markets do you serve?

We're headquartered in Kuwait and actively serve clients across Kuwait, Saudi Arabia, UAE, Egypt, Bahrain, Qatar, and Oman. Our cross-border execution network allows us to deliver campaigns across the entire Arab world.

Can Mashhor Hub help with AI and automation?

Absolutely. Through Mashhor Cloud — our proprietary AI layer — we offer AI-powered content creation, workflow automation, smart analytics, and operational acceleration tools that give your marketing a modern competitive edge.

How much do your services cost?

Our monthly management packages start from 150 KWD, with our most popular plan at 320 KWD. Enterprise and integrated packages are available for larger engagements. Visit our pricing page for full details, or book a call for a custom quote.

Do you offer training or courses?

Yes. Mashhor Academy provides free and premium courses across AI marketing, creative direction, automation workflows, media buying, and strategic execution — designed for marketers and business owners who want to level up.

How long does it take to see results?

This depends on the service and scope. Brand identity projects typically deliver in 2-4 weeks. Campaign results can be seen within the first month. For ongoing management, we provide monthly reporting so you can track progress from day one.

What if I'm not sure which service I need?

That's exactly what our free strategy call is for. We'll listen to your goals, assess your current position, and recommend the right combination of services or packages — with no pressure to commit.
