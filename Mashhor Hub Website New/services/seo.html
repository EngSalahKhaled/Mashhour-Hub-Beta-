Frequently Asked

Common questions about SEO with Mashhor Hub.

How long does it take to see SEO results?

Structural changes can improve indexing within weeks, but significant movements in ranking and organic traffic typically require 3 to 6 months of consistent optimization.

Do you optimize for both Arabic and English?

Yes. Search behavior differs greatly between Arabic and English speakers in the region. We map distinct keyword strategies for each language rather than just translating terms.

Is SEO better than paid ads?

They serve different purposes. Ads give immediate visibility but stop when you stop paying. SEO builds long-term digital asset value and compounding traffic that you own.

Do you rewrite my website content for SEO?

Yes, but we balance the need for search engine visibility with the need for high-quality, conversion-focused language for human readers.
