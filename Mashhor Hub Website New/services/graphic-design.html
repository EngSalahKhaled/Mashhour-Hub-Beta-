Frequently Asked

Common questions about design with Mashhor Hub.

Do you design full brand identities from scratch?

Yes. We build complete identity systems including logo, typography, color palettes, visual language, and application guidelines for new and established brands.

Can you refresh an existing brand?

Absolutely. We often modernize existing identities without losing their acquired equity, aligning them with digital-first usage and modern aesthetic standards.

What deliverables do I get at the end?

You receive all original vector files (Ai, EPS, SVG), usage guidelines, color codes, typography files, and exported assets ready for web, print, and social media.

Do you do ongoing design support?

Yes, many clients retain us for ongoing campaign design, ensuring every post, presentation, and ad matches the high standard of the core identity.
