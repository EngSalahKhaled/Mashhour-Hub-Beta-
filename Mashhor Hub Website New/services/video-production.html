Frequently Asked

Common questions about production with Mashhor Hub.

What kind of video production do you specialize in?

We focus on commercial video: social media reels, promotional campaign films, digital ads, and high-quality founder/brand story interviews.

Do you handle scriptwriting and concepts?

Yes. Our content team works directly with the video unit to develop the hooks, scripts, and narrative structure before the shoot.

How many variations do I get from a shoot?

We typically deliver a master edit and multiple platform-specific cuts (e.g., 9:16 for Reels/TikTok, shorter versions for ads). The exact deliverables are planned in the brief.

Can you shoot outside of Kuwait?

Yes. While our primary teams are based in Kuwait and Egypt, we actively coordinate and execute shoots across the UAE and Saudi Arabia.
