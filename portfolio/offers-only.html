The Challenge

A deals platform inherently operates on high volume and thin margins. Offers Only required a media buying strategy that achieved an exceptionally low Cost Per Acquisition (CPA) to remain profitable.

The Strategy

We executed aggressive AI-powered A/B testing across ad creatives and relied on dynamic catalog sales (DCA) to match the right deal with the right consumer behavior in real-time.

The Results

3x Transactions
-55% CPA

Customer acquisition cost dropped by over 55%, volume of transactions tripled during major seasonal sales, and the platform securely cemented its status as a go-to destination for local discounts.
