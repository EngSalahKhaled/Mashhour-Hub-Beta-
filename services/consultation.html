Frequently Asked

Common questions about consultations with Mashhor Hub.

Who is this consultation for?

Founders, marketing directors, and business owners who feel their current marketing isn't working, are planning a major launch, or need a second opinion on a high-budget campaign.

Is this just a sales call in disguise?

No. Our qualification calls are free and meant to determine if we are a fit. A paid strategy consultation is a standalone service where we deliver concrete, actionable value regardless of whether you hire us for execution.

What do I need to prepare?

We'll send you a brief questionnaire beforehand. Depending on your needs, you may want to prepare current ad data, brand guidelines, or past campaign results for review.

If I hire you for execution after, does the consultation fee apply?

In many cases, if a strategic consultation turns into a comprehensive retainer or large-scale project within 30 days, we credit a portion of the consultation fee toward the execution budget.
