Frequently Asked

Common questions about content writing with Mashhor Hub.

Is your Arabic content translated from English?

No. We believe literal translation destroys conversion. We perform transcreation — taking the core concept and writing it from scratch in a way that feels native to the Arab consumer.

Do you write for specific local dialects?

Yes. Depending on the campaign target, we can write in modern standard Arabic (Fusha), Kuwaiti dialect, Saudi dialect, Egyptian, or a universal "white" dialect that works across the GCC.

Can you assist with long-form SEO content?

Yes. We build blog content and editorial pieces designed specifically to rank for high-value search terms while maintaining professional quality.

Will you adapt the copy for different social platforms?

Absolutely. A caption on LinkedIn requires a different structure and tone than a caption for Instagram Reels. We adapt the source message for the specific environment.
